rules

  • Number

    Choose the number of moles you have to hit until you win.

  • Difficulty

    Select a gamemode by clicking on an animal. They represent the different gamemodes.

  • Start the game

    Click on the image on the left. A window pops up and you can start playing. After you have won the window disapears after some seconds.

  • Gameplay

    1. It is your goal to feed as much moles as possible.

    2. The moles appear and disappear randomly based on the gamemode you have chosen before.

    3. If you have fed a mole it wont disapear sad, but happy. After you have fed as much as needed you win.
