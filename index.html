rules

  • Prepare

    Choose a gamemode below the gameboard.

  • SinglePlayer

    Select the cross or the circle on the right side after you choose the gamemode. You beginn.

  • MultiPlayer

    Like before, choose a sign on the right side and start playing. On the left side you can see who`s turn it is and when you won.

  • Gameplay

    1. Two players alternate marking empty squares.

    2. If one player places three of the same marks in a row, that player wins.

    3. If the spaces are all filled and there is no winner, the game ends in a draw.
